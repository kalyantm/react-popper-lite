**topLeft** | **topCenter** | **topRight** | **rightTop** | **rightCenter** | **rightBottom** | **bottomRight** | **bottomCenter** | **bottomLeft** | **leftBottom** | **leftCenter** | **leftTop**

# Popper Triggers

The Popper can be triggered in multiple ways:

- **Mouse Events** (onMouseEnter, onMouseLeave)
- **Click Events** (onClick)
- **Focus Events** (onFocus, onBlur)

By default, it is triggered by mouse events.

# Controlled Popper

The Popper can also be used as a controlled component, by passing in various callback props, including:

- **isOpen**
- **onOpen**
- **onClose**
- **onMouseEnter**
- **onMouseLeave**
- **onClick**
- **onFocus**
- **onBlur**

The example below illustrates custom actions that are taken _onOpen_ and _onClose_ of the popper, like showing a toast.

# Colored Poppers

The Popper supports the entire range of colors! Choose what fits your needs or style, or preferably both &#128526;

- Specify the _color_ prop as the **name** of the color (eg. _red_, _blue_, etc), or the **hex** value (eg. **#8a9a5b**) or **rgb** (eg. **rgb(242, 133,0)**)
